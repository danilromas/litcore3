## FAQ (5)

1. **What does the subscription cover?** Hosting, model usage (tokens), monitoring, fixes and continuous improvements — plus human support. Your system is never left unattended after delivery.
2. **Why setup + subscription instead of a one-off fee?** An AI system is a live service: it answers daily, consumes infrastructure and needs tuning. Setup builds it; the subscription keeps it performing.
3. **How long does delivery take?** Bots and simple automations — days; SME websites and full processes — weeks. Scope and timeline are fixed in writing before we start.
4. **Can we order just the website?** Yes. Websites are a one-off budget: landing €300–2,000, SME site €1,500–4,000. Subscriptions apply only where AI runs.
5. **How do you scope a custom engagement?** By processes, not padded hours: which process, how many integrations, languages and channels. You get an itemised breakdown with the same public ranges before committing.
