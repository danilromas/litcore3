## FAQ

1. **Does it use our templates and price lists?**
   Yes — that is the foundation. We configure the system around your templates, pricing and house format.
2. **What if the AI makes a mistake?**
   Nothing is sent automatically. Every AI output is a draft reviewed and approved by a person on your team. Human oversight is built in — and required by our compliance approach.
3. **Is this compliant with EU rules on AI?**
   Yes. AI-generated content is labelled, users interacting with AI are informed (AI Act art. 50), and a human approves every outgoing document.
4. **What about our data (GDPR)?**
   Privacy by design: data processing agreement (art. 28 GDPR), data minimisation and role-based access. We review together what is processed, where and for how long before any real data flows.
5. **What about e-invoicing requirements (e.g. Verifactu in Spain)?**
   Certified invoicing remains with your invoicing system; we prepare and structure the data feeding it. In Spain, mandatory real-time reporting has been postponed to 2027 — we help you get there with clean data, without false urgency.
6. **How long does implementation take?**
   We start with a pilot on one document type — weeks, not months. You see results on your own data before scaling up.
